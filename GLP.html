Portfolio – Gur Ladizhinsky


Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Gur, and I’ve been making games for around 9 years now. This portfolio will highlight my favourite ones, alongside a list of (most) of the games I’ve worked on.

Most of my games were made in Unity, but I’ve experimented with other engines as well – Godot especially is one I’ve grown increasingly fond of. My favourite genres (and thus the ones I have the most experience with) are platformer, strategy, and RPG games, but I’m fine with working on any game so long as it’s not overly violent, suggestive and/or offensive. I love story-based games.
